📚
StudyHub User Guide

Everything you need to know to get the most out of StudyHub — your AI-powered study companion.

🤖 AI Powered 📊 Progress Tracking 👥 Study Groups ⏱️ Pomodoro Timer 🏆 Leaderboard
🚀 Getting Started
1
📝 Create Your Account
Click Sign Up and fill in your username, email and password. Your account is stored permanently — it will never be deleted when the app updates.
2
📁 Create Your First Subject
Go to Subjects in the navbar and click New Subject. Give it a name like "Mathematics" or "Biology". Subjects organize all your notes.
📝 Managing Notes
3
📄 Write Notes Manually
Open any subject and click New Note. Write your content, set a progress level (Unread / In Progress / Mastered) and save. You can also pick a color for each note!
4
📤 Export Notes to PDF
Open any note and click Export PDF to download a professionally formatted PDF of your note — great for printing or sharing!
🤖 AI Features
5
✨ AI Note Generator
Go to AI Notes in the navbar. Type any topic (e.g. "Newton's Laws"), pick your level and click Generate Note. The AI writes a full study note for you instantly!
💡 Tip Use the chat box below to ask follow-up questions about the topic.
6
🃏 AI Flashcards
Open any note and click Flashcards. The AI generates question-and-answer cards from your note content. Flip through them to test your knowledge!
7
🧠 AI Mind Map
Open any note and click Mind Map. The AI creates a visual mind map of the topic showing key connections and branches.
8
🗓️ AI Study Plan
From the dashboard click AI Study Plan. Enter your subjects and exam dates and the AI generates a personalized daily study schedule.
⚡ Productivity Tools
9
⏱️ Pomodoro Timer
Click Timer in the navbar. The Pomodoro technique breaks study time into 25-minute focused sessions with 5-minute breaks. Earn points for completing sessions!
10
📅 Exam Countdown
Click Exams in the navbar. Add your upcoming exam dates and the app shows a live countdown so you always know how many days are left!
👥 Social & Groups
11
👥 Study Groups
Click Groups in the navbar. Create a group and share the invite code with friends. Group members can view each other's notes and study together!
12
🏆 Leaderboard
Click Leaderboard to see how you rank against other students. Earn points by creating notes, completing Pomodoro sessions and mastering topics!
🔐 Account & Settings
13
👤 Your Profile
Click your name in the navbar → My Profile. Upload a profile photo, set an avatar color and write a short bio. Your stats are displayed here too.
14
🌙 Dark Mode
Click the 🌙 moon button in the top-right of the navbar to switch between light and dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
💡 Pro Tips
⭐ Mark notes as "Mastered" to track your progress and earn more points
📌 Pin important notes so they appear at the top of your subject
🔍 Use the search bar to quickly find any note or subject
🤖 AI works best with specific topics — the more specific, the better the note
👥 Share your invite code with classmates to build a study group
🔔 Check notifications regularly for updates and reminders
